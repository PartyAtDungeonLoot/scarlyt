How is Scarlyt played?

Scarlyt features 6 unique decks—Buildings, Spells, Creatures, Artifacts, Heroes, and Bonus—each containing 7 distinct cards, totaling 35 cards per deck. Players take turns rolling a six-sided die and drawing from the corresponding deck. The goal is to battle your opponents, defend your position, and collect cards for your hand and the board.
